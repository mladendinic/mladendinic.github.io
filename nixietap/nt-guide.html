A key function is under development, so the guide will come at a later date.

Until then, here are a few things that you can try.

  1. Press the button on the back of the enclosure
  2. Using your laptop or phone, do a WiFi scan
  3. Connect to the NixieTap network, default password is NixieTap
  4. (if a login page doesn't show up, navigate to 192.168.4.1. from your browser)
Now you're on the hotspot page, where you adjust all settings. For now it looks awful, but it will look better soon :)
  1. For accurate GMT time, go to Configure WiFi, and select the network that Nixie Tap should use. When you click Save, the hotspot will terminate
  2. To set GMT offset, push the button again, connect to NixieTap, go to Setup, and in the Semi-auto settings, write your desired time zone offset and DST
  3. Once you're done, press Back on your browser and click the Exit button on the hotspot
